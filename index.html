Adaptive trigers

Experience varying levels of strength and tension as you interact with your gear and your in-game environment. From pulling a very tight string to slamming on the brakes of a speeding car, feel physically connected to your on-screen actions.

Haptic Feedback

Feel physically responsive feedback to your in-game actions with dual actuators which replace traditional rumble motors. In your hands, these dynamic vibrations can simulate the feeling of everything from environments to the recoil of different weapons.
